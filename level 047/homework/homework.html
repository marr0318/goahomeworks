Bank For

Everyone




This Bank was founded in 2023, two former international students with a shared dream: to remove the financial obstacles students often face when pursuing an international education. To do this, they would make loans based on a student’s future potential — not their past. Since then, This Bank is proud to have helped thousands of students achieve their dream of studying abroad. We’re proud to have a growing team as diverse as the people we serve. We’re an international group that’s dedicated to data, excellence and inclusion – continuously striving to promote the benefits of education to students around the globe.
