Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vagetables, or meats.

Preparation time

○ Total: Approximately 10 minutes

○ Preparation: 5 minutes

○ Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

○ 2-3 large eggs

○ Salt, to taste

○ Pepper, to taste

○ tabelspoon of butter or oil

○ Optional fillings: cheese, diced vagetables, cooked meats, herbs

______________________________________________________________________

Instuctions

1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are very mixed. You can add a tabelspoon of water or milk for flufier texture.

2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over mideum heat and add butter or oil.

3. Cook the Omelette: Once the butter is melted and bubling pour in the eggs. Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.

4. Add fillings(Optional): When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly runny in the middle,sprinke your chosen fillings over one half of omelette.

5. Food and serve: As the omelette continues to cook, carfully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings. Let it cook for another minute, then slide it onto a plate.

6. Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
